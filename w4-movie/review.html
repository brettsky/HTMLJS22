Review

Brett Berard Feburary 7,2022

Interstellar is a fantastic movie that uses space travel to convey a strong message about human connection. This movie strikes a good balance between drama and action.

Although this movie is definelty more focused on the characters and their emotions it still has very suspensful action scenes that make the movie very intresting to watch. The suspense created throughout the movie

One thing that truly makes this movie amazing is the soundtrack to it. Hans Zimmer created an amazing soundtrack the perfectly fits the theme of space.

Trivia

  • Anne Hathaway suffered from hypothermia while filming in iceland, due to the fact that her astronaut suit was open while filming scenes in the icey water
  • The books displayed on the bookshelves, shown in this movie, were the actual books Nolan had read, and used, for his research about blackholes and singularity
  • The shape of the space station, is in reference to a clock face, with time being a major theme in the movie
  • The oft-quoted lines in the beginning with "Do not go gentler into that good night are from a poem by Dylan Thomas
