Interstellar is a sci-fi movie based around time. The movie uses themes of perseverance and human connection to create an emotional yet thrilling story. The main plot of the movie is about a group of scientists mission through a mysterious wormhole that recently appeared near Saturn. The group is going to explore the planets of previous scientists in order to find a planet that humanity can live on.
